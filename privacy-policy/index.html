Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Consonant ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use Ayah Island.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Onboarding data — your name and Quran learning goals, stored locally on your device.
  • Usage data — reading progress, streaks, and quiz results, stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase data — subscription status processed through Apple App Store and RevenueCat. We do not store payment details.

How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely to provide and improve the Ayah Island experience — personalising your learning journey, tracking progress, and managing your subscription.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — handles all payments and subscription management.
  • RevenueCat — processes subscription status. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Most data (name, progress, streaks) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat using an anonymous user identifier.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Ayah Island is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
